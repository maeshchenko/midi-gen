# Sample credits

Real-instrument samples used by the `REAL` playback mode.

## Pitched instruments — `guitar-electric/`, `bass-electric/`, `violin/`
From **tonejs-instruments** by Nicholaus Brosowsky.
- Repo: https://github.com/nbrosowsky/tonejs-instruments
- License: samples **CC-BY 3.0** (code MIT). Original sources: Freesound, Karoryfer, VSO.
- Attribution required: "Instrument samples from tonejs-instruments (CC-BY 3.0)".
- Re-encoded here to mono 128 kbps MP3 to keep the bundle light.

## Drums — `drums/metal/`
From the **Versilian Community Sample Library (VCSL)** by Versilian Studios.
- Repo: https://github.com/sgossner/VCSL
- License: **CC0 1.0** (public domain, no attribution required).
- Lanes: kick (Bass Drum 1), snare (Snare Drum Modern 1), hat/hatopen (Hi-Hat Cymbal),
  crash (Suspended Cymbal 1), tomhigh/tomlow (Tom 1/2), clap (Claps).
- Files are `lane_l{layer}_r{rr}.mp3`: velocity layers (l0=soft..) × round-robin
  variants (r0,r1..) — chosen per-hit to avoid the machine-gun effect.
- Re-encoded here to mono 128 kbps MP3.
